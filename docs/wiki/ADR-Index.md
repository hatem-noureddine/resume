## What are ADRs?

ADRs document significant architectural decisions, their context, and consequences. They help future contributors understand why certain choices were made.

## ADR Template

When creating a new ADR:

```markdown
# ADR-XXX: Title

## Status
Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded

## Context
Why is this decision needed?

## Decision
What was decided?

## Consequences
What are the implications?
```
